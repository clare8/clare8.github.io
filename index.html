Irresponsible Adoption

Every pet needs a loving owner, but not every owner is a good fit. My name is Clare Rigney and, for our assignment, my group takes a look at people in Montgomery County, Virginia who are helping stray and feral animals, as well as what animal lovers should know before adopting.

Video

It is a vastly different experience having a pet as a child as opposed to adopting one as an adult. The members of our group share personal stories about their experiences with pets and the lessons they learned.

Podcast

In Montgomery County, Virginia, there are organizations that work to adopt animals out, keep strays healthy, maintain a controlled and healthy ecosystem of feral cats, and much more. Our group spoke to Marilyn Wheaton of the Montgomery County Animal Shelter and Betty Bartschmid of the Animal Hope Alliance about what they do to help.

Medium Blog

Stray animals present an ethical issue because the lack of care does not mean a lack of strays. Overpopulation is often an issue and, unless community members step up to the plate, it will continue to be an issue. In these four blog posts, I talk about the practical and the personal components of adoption and animal care.
